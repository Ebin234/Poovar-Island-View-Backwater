Boating

Boating through Poovar

Poovar Island View Backwater is a charming boating club situated in the serene and picturesque locale of Poovar. Nestled among tranquil backwaters, this club offers an idyllic escape for nature enthusiasts and boating aficionados alike. Guests can enjoy leisurely boat rides through scenic waterways, taking in lush greenery and vibrant local wildlife. With its relaxed atmosphere and stunning views, Poovar Island View Backwater is the perfect destination for a peaceful retreat and an unforgettable experience in the heart of Kerala’s natural beauty.

Key Features

Discover Poovar's serene backwaters with our guided tours. Choose from country boats, Shikara boats, or speed boats and customize your sunrise, sunset, or full-day package. Enjoy expert commentary, fresh seafood, and local specialties at our in-house restaurant with backwater views. Private dining and jetty facilities ensure a seamless experience."

Kayaloram Stay

Poovar Island View Backwater offers a serene homestay experience in the picturesque village of Poovar, renowned for its enchanting backwaters. Our homestay features comfortable, well-appointed rooms with stunning views of the lush surroundings and tranquil waters. Guests can enjoy authentic local cuisine and personalized service that makes every stay memorable.In addition to cozy accommodations, Poovar Island View Backwater provides exclusive access to our backwater boating club. Whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure, our homestay promises a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural immersion.

Key Features

Experience the best of Poovar at our home stay, with private balconies overlooking the backwaters, air-conditioned rooms, and modern amenities. Enjoy authentic Kerala cuisine at our in-house restaurant, with stunning views and private dining options. Relax by the pool, stay connected with free Wi-Fi, and explore nearby attractions like Padmanabhapuram Palace and Kanyakumari. We cater to special dietary needs and ensure a comfortable, memorable stay
